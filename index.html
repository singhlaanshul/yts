Frequently Asked Questions

Q:

How much does Primer cost?

A:

Nothing. The app is totally free.

Q:

Who’s Primer for?

A:

Anyone looking to learn more about marketing -- from startup founders to small business owners to brand managers at Fortune 500 companies.

Q:

What topics do you cover?

A:

We provide lessons on topics like branding, storytelling, remarketing, SEO, and programmatic ad buying. In short, everything a marketer needs to be successful.

Q:

How often will you update the app?

A:

We’re releasing new lessons every week. Technical improvements and new features will be released periodically.

Q:

Who creates the lessons in Primer?

A:

First we look for the best explainers we can find. Then our creative team works with these experts to “Primerize” their knowledge, putting it into the Primer voice and thinking up interactions that enhance the learning experience.

Q:

I have an idea for a lesson. How can I get involved?

A:

We’d love to hear from you. Drop us a line at hello@yourprimer.com.
